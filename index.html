Background

About Me

I am an Assistant Lecturer at South East Technological University (SETU), Ireland, with a PhD in Microelectronics from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. My work spans analog/mixed-signal IC design, power electronics, RF circuits, and AI-enabled systems.

I bring hands-on expertise across the full IC design lifecycle — from schematic entry and simulation to layout, verification, and chip measurement — backed by three successful tape-outs in TSMC 65nm and GlobalFoundries 130nm SiGe BiCMOS processes.

Projects

Research Projects

Key research areas spanning mm-Wave ICs, power conversion, and 5G phased-array systems.

Time-Based PID Controller for Class-D Audio Power Amplification

Developed time-based PI control for advanced PWM generation in high-frequency buck converters, minimising output ripple voltage.

27–39 GHz Dual-Band Differential VCO for Mobile 5G

Bandwidth-enhanced differential LC-VCO and quadrature VCO supporting non-harmonically-related frequency bands in 0.13μm BiCMOS.

6-bit K-Band SiGe BiCMOS Digital Step Attenuator

A 6-bit 0.13μm SiGe BiCMOS DSA with low phase variation for K-band and Ka-band applications, with full tape-out verification.

6-bit 38 GHz SiGe BiCMOS Phase Shifter for 5G

High-precision phase shifter targeting 5G phased-array communication systems, designed and verified in IEICE Electronics Express.

CMOS Dual-Feedback Reconfigurable LNA

Low noise amplifier with improved stability and reduced noise figure using dual-feedback reconfigurable topology in CMOS process.

5-bit SOI CMOS Digital Step Attenuator

Silicon-on-insulator CMOS DSA design targeting high-frequency switching and low insertion loss for RF front-end applications.

AI-Enabled Captioning System for Visually Impaired

Real-time captioning and computer vision assistive system for blind and visually impaired persons — filed as a patent in 2026.

Geo-Aware RAG Chatbot for Walking Support

Prototype retrieval-augmented chatbot with geospatial awareness presented at ICHCAI 2026, Norway.
